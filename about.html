ABOUT US

"Life Is About Moments, Dont Wait For Them Infact Create Them"

Sweet Bakery is Pakistan’s leading and first American-styled bakery that is based in Lahore, Pakistan. Founded in 2010, the talented team of pastry chefs and sugar-craft artists is creating edible works of art for special occasions including weddings, baby showers, birthday parties, corporate events etc. Renowned for irresistibly pretty and elegant designs that are not only visually stunning but also taste delicious, Sweet Bakery has acquired an enviable client list including many celebrities, companies and members of high society.

Our Introduction

Sweet Bakery a subsidiary of Mini Foods is Pakistan's first American-style bakery. The company, which was founded in 2010, offers a full range of American-style baked treats and desserts including cakes, cupcakes, brownies, cheesecakes, cookies and much more. Sweet Bakery specialty lies in bespoke cakes and cupcakes that are made exclusively for distinctive events like weddings, birthday parties, corporate events and other special events. Our unique selling proposition is Our that we make everything fresh from the oven, whether its cakes, cupcakes or cookies. Our customer gets fresh product baked on the same respective day which makes us different and distinct from other companies companies selling similar stuff but without same day baking facility. Moreover, we have state-of-the-art facility with top-notch raw material procured from around the world to give customer an awe-inspiring experience with unique taste, which is not o which is not offered anywhere.
